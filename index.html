Welcome!

Our mission is to create a safe and enjoyable environment for patrons to be around and interact with our rescue cats. We want to provide animal companionship and high-quality beverages and services to those who might not have the luxury of owning their own pet. If you happen to feel let down or in dire need of companionship we offer those services and hope you would stop by to meet the cats!

Why did we do this?

Cat cafés have started becoming more popular in places all around the world, we wanted to create our own café here in Winnipeg. While the focus is to provide a safe and comfortable environment for our customers, we also want to provide a safe space for our cats, so the goal of this project is to offer a cozy and quaint café experience with high-quality beverages and dishes, to those who love interacting with cats.
